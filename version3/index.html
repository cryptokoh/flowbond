Common Questions

What is FlowBond?

FlowBond is a platform that combines wearable technology with blockchain to reward physical movement and dance.

How does earning work?

Wear the FlowBond device, move or dance, and automatically earn $DANZ tokens based on your activity level.

What blockchain is used?

FlowBond operates on the Solana blockchain for fast, efficient, and eco-friendly transactions.
